# Treasury Vessel — Reserves & Liquidity
**Public Codex File**  
**Domain: Capital • Reserves • Payouts**

## Treasury Vessel Identity
Vessel: Treasury Vessel  
Domain: Capital, reserves, liquidity, payouts  
Scope: Supports the Royal Capital architecture  

## Mandate
The Treasury Vessel:

- Holds reserves backing issued instruments  
- Manages liquidity across rails and venues  
- Coordinates payouts and redemptions  
- Aligns capital structure with governance principles  

## Separation of Authority
The Treasury Vessel does **not**:

- Declare issuance (Bank Vessel domain)  
- Provide truth signals (Oracle Vessel domain)  
- Cross into other vessel mandates  

Each vessel remains sovereign in its domain.

## Public‑Safe Scope
No reserve composition, allocation strategies, or internal policies are included.
