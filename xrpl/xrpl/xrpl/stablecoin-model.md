# Stablecoin Model — XRPL Layer
**Public Codex File**

## Purpose
The stablecoin model provides a **stable‑value representation** on XRPL.

## Characteristics
- **Defined Reference Unit:** Parity with a declared standard  
- **Controlled Issuance:** Only authorized channels expand supply  
- **Compliance‑Aware:** Transfers may reflect eligibility or policy state  
- **Ledger‑Visible:** Balances and flows are public  

## Vessel Roles
- **Bank Vessel:** Declares stablecoin legitimacy  
- **Treasury Vessel:** Holds reserves (off‑ledger)  
- **Oracle Vessel:** May provide reference data  

## Public‑Safe Scope
No reserve composition, risk models, or operational logic are included.
