# XRPL Issuance Model — Royal Capital Bank Vessel
**Public Codex File**

## Issuance Purpose
The XRPL layer represents **issued units** declared by the Bank Vessel.  
These units may represent:

- Stable value claims  
- Tokenized interests  
- Structured instruments  
- Real‑world asset representations  

## Issuance Principles
- **Declared by the Bank Vessel**  
- **Minted through controlled channels**  
- **Burned through authorized procedures**  
- **Visible on XRPL**  
- **Aligned with vessel separation**  

## Vessel Roles
- **Bank Vessel:** Declares issuance and legitimacy  
- **Treasury Vessel:** Manages reserves (off‑ledger)  
- **Oracle Vessel:** Provides verification signals  

## Public‑Safe Scope
No operational procedures or internal controls are included.
