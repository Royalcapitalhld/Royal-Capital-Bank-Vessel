# Assurance Framework Overview — Royal Capital Architecture
**Public Codex File**  
**Domain: Confidence • Verification • Structure**

## Purpose of Assurance
Assurance in the Royal Capital architecture refers to:

- Confidence in structure and process  
- Verification of certain conditions or states  
- Alignment between declared architecture and actual practice  

Assurance is **framework‑level**, not a guarantee of outcome.

## Assurance Layers (Conceptual)
- **Governance Assurance:** Structures, roles, and responsibilities.  
- **Operational Assurance:** Processes and controls (kept private).  
- **Technical Assurance:** Code review, testing, and verification (kept private).  
- **Jurisdictional Assurance:** Alignment with legal structures and filings.

## Vessel Roles
- **Bank Vessel:** Governance and issuance assurance.  
- **Treasury Vessel:** Capital and reserve assurance (off‑ledger).  
- **Oracle Vessel:** Verification and truth assurance.

## Public‑Safe Scope
No internal controls, audit procedures, or specific assurance reports are included.
